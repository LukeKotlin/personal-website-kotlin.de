Myself in 7 sentences

In general, I'm a self-taught web developer who started building websites at the age of 15. At the beginning, I only used Wordpress themes to build simple websites for family members or me. Later on I spent the most time on ranking somebody's website to the top on google rankings, but quickly realized that I want to build websites from scratch, combined with a good knowledge of SEO and marketing.

I would describe myself as visual person with great attention on design details. Usability is always the focus in my development process, to create the best possible experience and to generate the highest conversion rate.

Last but not least, I'm not the typical coffee drinking programmer. I drink water most of the time.

Workout App

The workout App was one of my first small side projects to improve my android development skills. Each workout lasts 30 seconds follows by a 10 second break. During the break the next exercise is announced by the media player of your phone. To exit the workout use the backbutton and a custom dialog will open. If you finish the exercise the finishing time will stored in a SQLite database, which you can find under history. Moreover you can use the BMI calculator to find out if you overweighted or underweighted.

github: https://github.com/LukeKotlin/workout-app

tech stack: android studio, kotlin, gradle, SQLite

Kotlin Tinder clone

A side project where I converted an existing java application into kotlin.

Kotlin Tinder clone

This application was one of my first side projects where I convertet an excisting java tinder clone into Kotlin. The app contains a login and a registration option. If you're using the app the first time you have to registrate yourself. After that your login data is stored in a database on firebase. Depending on which gender your choose in the registration the opposite gender is shown on the start screen. I used the SwipeCards libary to display the original swipe feature of Tinder. A short toast message will show you if you matched somebody else. Beside from just swiping you can also chat with your matches or edit your profile details for example your profile picture.

github: https://github.com/LukeKotlin/tinder-clone-kotlin

tech stack: android studio, java, kotlin, gradle, firebase

filmproduktion-duesseldorf.de & filmproduktion-muenchen.de

I built these two projects to get some practice in working with API's. I used the nearbySearch() and the getDetails() service from the Google Places API. Underneath the google map I placed the pagination of search results and the getDetails() data is displaying in the infowindow of each marker. Both sites are built with pure html, css and javaScript, no frameworks, no libraries. The next step is to place some Google Adsense advertisments on both pages to generate some passive income and to sell advertisment space to movie production companies in both cities.
